SP CDR Reconciliation Blockchain

Privacy-Preserving Settlement Architecture for Telecom Operators

Architecture Overview

The SP CDR Reconciliation Blockchain is a privacy-preserving settlement system that enables telecom operators to perform triangular netting of Call Detail Records (CDRs) without revealing sensitive customer data. The architecture consists of seven distinct layers, each providing specific functionality.
